Saba University

Since its founding in 1993, more than 1,500 physicians have earned their M.D. at Saba University (www.saba.edu). Saba University School of Medicine has been accredited by the Accreditation Commission on Colleges of Medicine (ACCM) and its program has received approvals from licensing boards in New York, California and Florida. The campus is on Saba, which is located very near St. Maarten.

Medical University of the Americas

Since its founding 1998, Medical University of the Americas (www.mua.edu) has awarded approximately 500 M.D.’s. The MUA program is accredited by the Accreditation Commission on Colleges of Medicine (ACCM) and its program has received approvals from the licensing board in New York. MUA is located on Nevis, near St. Kitts.

St. Matthew’s University

St. Matthew’s University (www.stmatthews.edu) offers both a medical and a veterinary program. Since 1997, almost 1,500 students have obtained their M.D. and D.V.M. degrees from St. Matthews. The program is accredited by the Accreditation Commission on Colleges of Medicine (ACCM). St. Matthews is located in the Cayman Islands.

Sierra Education Finance Corp.

Equinox Capital founded Sierra Education Finance Corp. to provide credit and financial support to providers of student financial aid and their loan programs, as well as direct financial aid to students.

Comprehensive NeuroScience, Inc.

Comprehensive NeuroScience, Inc. (CNS) (www.cnswebsite.com) is an integrated clinical research and knowledge development company specializing in neuropsychiatric and related disorders. CNS has the largest network of clinical research sites for central nervous system trials in the nation. Through its Drug Development and Clinical Trials divisions, CNS designs, implements and analyzes clinical development programs on behalf of pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies and contract research organizations. CNS implements these trials within its 18 owned and operated clinical trial sites across the U.S. CNS is increasingly working with its pharmaceutical customers, from early clinical development to market development of their drug therapies, allowing them to accelerate the availability and increase the appropriate use of marketable drug therapies.

Care Management Technologies

Care Management Technologies (CMT) provides an evidence-based suite of three highly efficient IT-based decision support products to improve the quality of care and reduce the cost of care for patients suffering from problems of behavioral health. These products address the three essential causes of inefficiency in the care of these patients: appropriate use of psychotropic medication; maintenance of adherence to medication; and integration of critical aspects of behavioral and general medical care. CMT’s programs have been implemented in 22 state Medicaid plans, several large private insurance plans and in the Canadian province of Manitoba.

Cushcraft Corporation

Cushcraft Corporation is a world leader in the design, manufacture, development, and marketing of high-performance communications antennas which address a variety of applications in frequencies ranging from 900MHz to 5.8GHz. Cushcraft’s products are utilized in Wireless Local Area Network, Wireless Local Loop, Wireless Internet, and Wireless Broadband applications utilizing WiFi, PCS/cellular, and other communications protocols.  Cushcraft was subsequently sold to Polaris Venture Partners and Riverside Partners, Inc. 

Diamond Communication Products, Inc.

Diamond Communication Products, Inc. is a leading manufacturer of pole line hardware for the telecommunications industry.  Diamond was grown through a series of add-on acquisitions, product line expansions and internal growth of the company’s core business.  Diamond was subsequently sold to Thomas & Betts Corporation.
