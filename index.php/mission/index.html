Tactical and strategic expertise to build growth, value and quality.

Equinox Capital takes special interest—and brings unique strengths—to the needs of well-positioned businesses that find themselves operating within complicated and often restrictive parameters. These may include difficult, multi-level legal situations; complicated, cross-jurisdictional tax and regulatory restrictions, as well as internal corporate governance issues.

In many cases, the companies we work with have a strong legacy of entrepreneurship in which these constraints impede the emergence of the management structure, processes and corporate environment needed to foster and sustain innovative, industry-leading performance. These issues can arise in the life cycle of nearly any enterprise and often are most pronounced during generational transitions.

Equinox Capital has a proven track record in resolving these challenges through sustained tactical and strategic support across a range of disciplines (finance, executive recruitment, marketing, etc.), all of it anchored in a strong operational orientation.
