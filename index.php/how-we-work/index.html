Proven closing ability and experience working successfully with management

In all of our investments, the partners of Equinox Capital relentlessly pursue every opportunity for business expansion and the achievement of value, including internal growth and add-on acquisitions. We take pride in our ability to creatively structure transactions to meet the needs of business owners, management and employees, as well as the needs of Equinox and its affiliated limited partners.

While we evaluate a range of considerations in making our investment decisions, a determining factor is the presence of a management team that understands the value that we provide and is poised to capitalize on our resources and expertise. Equinox principals become actively involved at the board level and have frequent contact with senior management.

In every engagement, we rely on our proven closing ability and experience working successfully with management to achieve our main objective of building and growing industry-leading companies.
